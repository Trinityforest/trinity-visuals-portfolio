ABOUT
TRINITY

I'm a visual storyteller based in Johannesburg — obsessed with the tension between light and shadow, the single frame that holds a thousand words, and the moving image that stops time.

Trinity Visuals bridges the worlds of photography and film. Whether I'm shooting a brand campaign, a cinematic wedding, a music video, or a documentary portrait — every frame is made with the same devotion.

My approach is quiet. Deliberate. I don't chase the expected shot. I wait for the honest one.
